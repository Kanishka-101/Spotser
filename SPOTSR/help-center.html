Frequently Asked Questions

Our services are available from 9 AM to 9 PM, seven days a week, including most public holidays. You can schedule bookings within these hours, subject to professional availability. Our customer support operates from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
All Spotsers professionals undergo a stringent multi-stage vetting process, including criminal background checks, skill assessments, and continuous training. We also collect customer feedback after every service to ensure consistent high quality and professional standards.
While our system primarily matches you with the best available professional for your needs, you can often request a preferred professional for recurring services, subject to their availability. You can manage this preference in your account settings.
Spotsers is committed to your satisfaction. In the rare event of an issue, please contact our customer support immediately. We have a robust resolution process in place, including insurance coverage for damages and re-service options, to ensure your peace of mind.
Spotsers currently operates in major cities across India, including Guwahati, Bangalore, Delhi, Mumbai, and Chennai. We are continuously expanding our service areas. You can check availability by entering your pincode during the booking process or on our service area page.
